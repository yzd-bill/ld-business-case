Business case for training or development

Use this form to request approval for training, a certification, conference, course or coaching. A strong case connects the investment to your role, to the business, and to the Data & Technology team. Fill it in, then print to PDF, copy it for email, or download it as a Word document to send for approval.

What you are requesting
1Relevance to your role
Explain how this connects to what you do day to day. Be specific about the skills it builds and the work it will directly improve.
  • What part of your role does this develop?
  • What can you not do well enough today that this addresses?
  • How soon would you apply it, and to which work or clients?
2Contribution to the business
Set out the return dentsu gets from this investment. Connect it to commercial outcomes, client value, quality, speed or risk where you can.
  • What business outcome does this support (revenue, margin, delivery quality, client retention, risk)?
  • Does it help win, grow or protect a client relationship? Name it if so.
  • What is the cost of not doing it?
3Contribution to the Data & Technology team
Describe how this strengthens the team and the wider practice, not just you. Strong cases build capability that others benefit from.
  • Which capability gap in the team or pillar does this help close?
  • How will you share what you learn (write-up, session, template, mentoring)?
  • Does it support a team priority, a new service, or a platform or tooling direction?
4Outcomes and measures of success
Say what will be true after the training that is not true now, and how you and your manager will know it worked.
  • What will you be able to do or produce that you cannot today?
  • How will the outcome be measured or evidenced?
  • By when?
